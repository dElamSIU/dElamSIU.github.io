Conditions

Equipment

All rentals come with a helmet, basket, and headlights. The equipment must be returned at the end of the rental period. Failure to return equipment will result in additional fees equivalent to cost of the lost missing equipment.

Payment

Payment will be received prior to the bikes being released for customer use. Additional fees for maintenance and missing equipment may be added at the end of the rental period if any heavy wear is apparent on the bike including: scratches in the paint, bent wheels and/or frame, damaged tires, and other damages that would compromise the bike.

Use

The bikes are to be used for standard use and wear. The bikes are not to be used in a way that would result in damage. Prohibited scenarios include: large jumps or falls, wheelies, and intentional grinding of the bike frame on a surface. Damages from apparent misuse will result in additional fees to be payed at the end of the rental period.
