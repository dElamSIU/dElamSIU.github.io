Midway Cinemas

Midway Cinemas is a franchise devoted to providing the lowest prices with the highest end equipment to bring the best movie watching experience possible to as many cities as possible.

We are among the first of the theatre franchises to receive movies the soonest from publishers to ensure that you will be able to watch your most highly anticipated movie sooner.

Some of the features we offer include:

  • Reclinable seats.
  • Top of the line projectors capable of displaying 4k movies for astounding detail.
  • top grade surround sound speakers delivering the best audio quality possible.
  • Snacks and beverages below the regular movie theatre price.
